Katherine Johnson was an American mathematician born in 1918. She worked at NASA as a brilliant mathematician, where she calculated important flight paths for space missions. In her work, Katherine helped calculate the trajectories for astronauts, which is why she is considered one of the most important figures in space exploration. She believed that math could help humans explore space, and she is an important role model for girls in STEAM.
